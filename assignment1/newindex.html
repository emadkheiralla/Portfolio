About Me

I am a passionate, hardworking professional, with over 16 years of experience in different aspects of Information Technology. I served eight wonderful years in the U.S. Navy as an IT where I was trained to be a network administrator, maintaining Unclassified, Secret and Top Secret computer networks. I was a Jr. Application developer at IBM, where I honed my web development skills. I worked as a Jr. Software Engineer for Web Head Technologies, subcontracted to the US Air Force's NIDSIII project, developing the front end of a Nuclear Weapons system. I am currently a Software Engineer for Schneider Electric working on the User Interface for Building Management Systems. I keep atop of new developments within the industry and can adapt quickly to new coding conventions. I am happy working independently or in a close team environment, and apply a positive attitude to every task I undertake.
